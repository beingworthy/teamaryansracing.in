About E-BAJA

E-BAJA is a national level off-road vehicle design competition, organized by SAEINDIA. Teams of students need to design and build an Electric powered ATV that can withstand the harshest impact of rough terrain pushing their boundaries in the roots of innovation. It also offers an opportunity for students to interact with industry professionals and potential employers, as well as to showcase their talent and innovation in automotive design and manufacturing.

About ATVC

ATVC (Aravalli Terrain Vehicle Championship) is a national-level event that serves as a platform exclusively designed for passionate engineering students from colleges nationwide. The event celebrates the hard work and dedication of student clubs who spend an entire year designing, manufacturing, managing finances, and marketing their vehicles.
